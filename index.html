Our Work

The Cherishapple Empowerment Foundation is a Nigerian organization that was established by Christian Obioma, also known as 'Mr Cherish Apple', and the Vice President, Mrs Evelyn Alaegbu. The purpose of this group is to provide teenagers with the necessary skills and resources to become successful and responsible members of the community. To reach this goal, CHEF focuses on six key areas: positive influence on society, leadership, integrity, godly influence, supporting and defending deaf and dumb children, and sheltering street kids. With its years of experience, CHEF is now aiming to become recognized globally.

GOAL 1

Teaching Teens how to affect the Society positively.

CHEF's primary aim is to instruct teenagers on how they can make a positive impact on their society. CHEF organizes workshops and seminars to equip young people with the knowledge and capabilities they need to be engaged members of their communities. Through these activities, teens understand how they can employ their gifts and capabilities to bring about change in the world around them.

Monthly writing contests is involved.

Goal 2

Leadership.

CHEF's aim is to equip teens with the necessary abilities for leadership. Mentorship programs provided by CHEF give teenagers the assurance and knowledge they need to be successful. Teens are taught how to be proactive, create objectives, run projects, and work together with others.

Goal 3

Integrity.

CHEF's objective is to foster youth with integrity. We provide activities for character development, such as community service and ethical decision-making, which will help young people form strong moral principles to guide them in the future.
